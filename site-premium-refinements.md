# Premium Refinement Pack for advanceapractice.com

This implementation-ready pack preserves the existing substance while improving clarity, structure, visual quality, and conversion confidence.

## 1) Heading and messaging upgrades

### Global rule
- Use one **H1** per page.
- Keep H1 between **4–8 words**.
- Add one concise subhead sentence under H1 describing audience + outcome.

### Immediate headline replacements

| Page | Current direction | Recommended H1 | Recommended subhead |
|---|---|---|---|
| Home | Broad, long-form headline | **Operational support for behavioral-health practices** | Stabilize reimbursement, reduce denials, and simplify the work between clinical documentation and paid claims. |
| Mental Health Billing | Highly descriptive and long | **Steadier behavioral-health billing** | For therapy, psychiatry, and PMHNP practices that need cleaner claims flow and more predictable collections. |
| Medical Billing | Function-forward but generic | **Outpatient billing that works** | Improve claim quality, lower preventable rework, and strengthen month-to-month revenue consistency. |
| Credentialing | Process detail-heavy | **Credentialing that speeds provider readiness** | Get providers enrolled correctly and on time so visits convert into reimbursable care faster. |

### Section hierarchy template (all service pages)
- **H1:** page headline
- **H2:** Who it’s for
- **H2:** Problems we solve
- **H2:** What improves
- **H2:** How engagement works
- **H2:** Proof
- **H2:** Next step

## 2) Copy refinements (substance preserved)

### “Benefit-first” summary box pattern
Place directly beneath the hero on service pages.

**Example (Mental Health Billing):**
> For therapy, psychiatry, and PMHNP practices facing recurring denials, documentation-billing disconnects, and delayed follow-up. We stabilize reimbursement and make billing easier to manage day to day.

### Paragraph compression standard
- Cap paragraphs at **2–3 sentences**.
- Convert long diagnostic/problem lists into bullets.
- Keep each bullet focused on one clear operational issue or outcome.

### Keyword placement standard
Use target phrases naturally in the first 150 words and in at least one H2 where relevant:
- behavioral health billing
- provider credentialing
- revenue cycle management
- denial reduction
- claim follow-up

## 3) Layout and structure upgrades

### Card-based content blocks
Use three-card rows for core sections:
1. Problems we solve
2. What improves
3. Deliverables

**Card anatomy**
- Icon
- 3–5 word title
- 1 sentence explanation
- Optional metric/proof line

### Proof placement
Move social proof and evidence immediately after the summary box on each service page:
- John Benson testimonial in styled quote block
- Reimbursement-thread screenshot in bordered callout with short caption

### Consolidate repeated “provider pathways” content
Create a single **Solutions by Stage** hub page and replace repeated blocks with short intros + links:
- New practice setup
- Growth-stage optimization
- Recovery from billing instability

## 5) Premium visual system (light-touch)

### Typography pairing
- Headings: serif (e.g., *Merriweather* or *Fraunces*)
- Body/UI: sans-serif (e.g., *Inter* or *Source Sans 3*)

### Spacing
- Increase section padding to 72–96px desktop / 48–64px mobile.
- Use max-width containers (1120–1200px) for editorial readability.

### Color palette
- Deep navy for primary text/headings
- Teal for CTAs and active states
- Warm light gray for alternate section backgrounds

## 6) Interaction polish and accessibility

### Motion system
- Fade-in on section entry (short duration)
- Slight lift on card hover
- Smooth accordion transitions

### Accessibility guardrails
- Respect `prefers-reduced-motion: reduce`
- Maintain visible focus states
- Ensure color contrast meets WCAG AA
