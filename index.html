Frequently asked questions

Do you replace our EHR or billing software?

No. We work inside what you already use — SimplePractice, TherapyNotes, AdvancedMD, Kareo, and similar. We change how the work flows, not the tools you pay for.

How fast can we start?

Most engagements begin within 1–2 weeks after a discovery call. Credentialing timelines depend on payer response, but our prep work typically shortens the clock by 4–8 weeks.

How do you handle PHI?

No patient PHI is shared in the first contact or on this site. Once a BAA is in place, we operate inside your systems under your access controls.

What's the investment range?

Engagements are scoped to provider count, payer mix, and backlog depth. We share a written scope with ranges before anything starts.
