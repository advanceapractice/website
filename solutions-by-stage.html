Solutions by stage

Most billing breakdowns happen at one of three stages. Pick the one that matches where your practice actually is right now.

How to use this page: Find the stage below that describes your current reality, then jump to the relevant service. We match the work to the stage — no generic retainer.

Stage 01 · New practice setup

You're launching, adding a new entity, or spinning up a second location. The goal is to stand up billing and credentialing correctly the first time.

✓

Clean enrollment

CAQH setup, payer paneling, and W-9 / taxonomy alignment so claims don't get rejected at day one.

✓

Workflow scaffolding

Intake → documentation → claim → follow-up built as one system, not three disconnected ones.

✓

Reporting from day one

Weekly reporting so early volume issues surface before they become financial.

Stage 02 · Growth-stage optimization

You're billing, but denials, A/R aging, or documentation gaps are quietly costing you. You need operational tightening, not a rip-and-replace.

✓

Denial reduction

Pattern-level fixes for the same 3–5 denials that repeat month after month.

✓

Follow-up cadence

Payer-specific follow-up so nothing ages past timely filing, and nothing sits with no owner.

✓

Usable reporting

A short weekly summary that surfaces decisions: hire, reschedule, re-sequence, escalate.
